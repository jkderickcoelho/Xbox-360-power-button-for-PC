Xbox Style PC Power Button Project

This project lets you use a custom button to:
- Turn your PC on
- Play a startup sound using a DFPlayer Mini

All wiring diagrams and files are included in this folder.

Watch the full video for assembly and wiring instructions.

https://youtu.be/q-6wddOrkQA 


WHEN YOU MAKE THE (2X8 CM) BOARD FOR THE BUTTON, CUT IT TO FIT WITHIN THE 2 INCH DIAMETER HOLE AFTER ESTABLISHING YOUR METAL PEG POINTS YOU WILL SOLDER IT DOWN WITH, (once board is on, its on forever and you wont be able to put it through your hole you cut)
